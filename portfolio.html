Underneath, I have put some of my experiences with tinkering, mostly in the shape of projects I have done throughout my life so far.


car

Date: Long, long ago...


When I was 7, I suddenly got the great idea to build a woorden car...

So I did.
It took me many years, and was one of the first big projects I ever did. I also didn't make any plans or designs or whatever: I just got some wood, screws and a drill and got working :)

ring

Date: Somewhere in 2020


I'm a big fan of The Lord of the Rings, and one day wanted to make a ring myself. So I got a nut in the shed, some sanding paper, files and metal saws and got working. Making the ring took around 20 hours, and I still wear it to this day.
